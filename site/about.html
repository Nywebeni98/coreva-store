Who We Are

Coreva Online Store is a South African e-commerce store dedicated to making premium fragrances, skincare and everyday household essentials accessible to everyone. We believe that quality products shouldn't come with unreachable price tags.

Our Mission

We curate the best combo deals so you get more value for your money. From signature perfumes to household must-haves, every product in our store is handpicked to ensure quality and affordability.

Why Shop With Us?

We keep it simple — great products, honest prices, and a shopping experience designed around you. All prices are in South African Rand (ZAR) with no hidden fees or surprises.

Our Promise

We are committed to bringing you genuine products at the best possible prices. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we work hard every day to earn your trust and keep you coming back.
